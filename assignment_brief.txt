Assignment Brief

Design a poster-format graphic design work that visually communicates a cultural or conceptual theme. The work should translate the assigned theme into a clear visual message through layout, typography, color, imagery, and overall craft.

The design should be evaluated according to the following considerations:

1. Visual hierarchy and layout
The poster should organize information clearly and guide the viewer's attention through deliberate use of composition, spacing, alignment, scale, and balance.

2. Typography
Typeface choice, typographic hierarchy, spacing, and readability should support the message and tone of the work.

3. Color
The color palette should be coherent, provide appropriate contrast, and contribute to the visual meaning and hierarchy of the poster.

4. Concept and communication
The main idea should be understandable from the visual choices. The concept should be communicated clearly and, where possible, show originality in how the theme is translated into visual language.

5. Craft and finish
The final work should show careful execution, consistency, and attention to detail, with no unintended visual or production errors.

The goal of the assignment is not only to produce an attractive poster, but to communicate a culturally or conceptually meaningful idea through visual design decisions.
